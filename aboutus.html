About Company

Travel to Uzbekistan means an acquaintance with another world rich with its turquoise domes and grandiose scenery, cherished traditions and open hospitable people.

Travel agency ORIENT VOYAGES is the leading tour operator for inbound tourism in Uzbekistan, with its head office in Samarkand and TashkentÂ  representative office.

The company was founded in December 1992 and became one of the first private companies in the tourist market of independent Uzbekistan. The purpose of the company was to provide a wide range of travel services for staying in Uzbekistan and other Central Asian countries for individual and group travelers.

Providing quality services

Over the years we have developed strong partnerships with suppliers of travel services (hotels, restaurants, museums, railways and airlines, etc.), which allows us to offer our customers competitive prices and high quality service.

An important factor in providing quality services is that Orient Voyages has its own team of highly qualified guides and interpreters.

Well-developed infrastructure

The list of our partners includes major travel companies in Europe and the Middle East, Australia and Southeast Asia. We are proud of long-term cooperation with them.

For many years the company has expanded its operations and now has a well developed infrastructure. We have created a network of hotels ORIENT STAR in the main tourist centers of the country - Khiva, Bukhara and Samarkand, and its own fleet of modern buses and minibuses ORIENT BUS.

Orient Voyages is a member of various tourism fairs

Several years ago we had a reconstruction of an old caravanserai in Samarkand, which now hosts a variety of events for our tourists: evenings with folk performances, traditional tea parties, familiarizing tourists with recipes for oriental sweets and national dishes.

Orient Voyages is a permanent member of various tourism fairs - ITB (Germany), Top Resa (France), TTG Incontri (Italy), ITB Asia (Singapore), WTM (UK), FITUR (Spain).

Experienced and professional multi-lingual team

Our experienced and professional multi-lingual team is always ready to make up the best route of travel taking into account your wishes and travel time.

We will provide all necessary information to each client from the rules of visa support to the organization of the most diverse and interesting tours in Uzbekistan and other Central Asian countries.
