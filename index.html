About Us

Travel agency ORIENT VOYAGES is the leading tour operator for inbound tourism in Uzbekistan

The company was founded in December 1992 and became one of the first private companies in the tourist market of independent Uzbekistan.

The purpose of the company was to provide a wide range of travel services for staying in Uzbekistan and other Central Asian countries for individual and group travelers.
